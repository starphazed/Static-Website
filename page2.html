Yin and Yang

Yin and Yang originate from ancient Chinese philosophy and are a foundational concept in Taoism. They represent the dual forces of nature that exist in balance within the universe. The symbolism of Yin and Yang aligns closely with the number 2, as both emphasize the idea of opposites working in harmony to form a whole. Yin, associated with darkness, femininity, and passivity, is balanced by Yang, representing light, masculinity, and activity. In Chinese culture, this duality illustrates how seemingly opposing forces are interconnected and interdependent, much like the number 2 symbolizes the relationship between complementary elements.
